Our Mission

As New York City high school students, we want to raise awareness about the prevalent issue of violence in NYC schools through this website. As many are unfamiliar with the efforts taken by the government and other organizations to stop violence, there is a need to provide more details and support to the community. In order to reach more people, we need you to share our website. Our main goal is to implement more mediations across New York City schools to prevent the escalation of violence and give support to those who need or desire it.
