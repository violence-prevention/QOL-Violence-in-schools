Resources

Violence in schools is a real problem that needs to be addressed by professionals. If you have been a victim use these resources to seek help. You can also use these resources to learn more about the cause.

Screenshot taken from the Sandy Hook Promise website. Sandy Hook Promise

This is an initiative towards safer schools, advocating against guns and educating people on ways to prevent severe tragedies. The Sandy Hook promise is a national non-profit which was founded by a family whose loved ones were killed in the 2012 Sandy Hook Elementary School shooting.

Screenshot taken from the NYC Public Schools website. Respect For All

This is a NYC Department of Education initiative that promotes a supportive, inclusive, and safe school environment. By prohibiting violent behavior such as bullying and discrimination, it seeks to build a united and respectful community within schools. With this, school personnel can feel comfortable connecting and obtain a quality education.

Screenshot taken from the KAVI website. Kings Against Violence Initiative

The Kings Against Violence Initiative is a non-profit organization based in Brooklyn. It's mission is to prevent and potentially eliminate interpersonal and youth violence in fundamental spaces such as schools and hospitals as well as the community as a whole. For schools, they have programs which operate in Central Brooklyn schools and provide mentorship, conflict resolution training, and other support to avoid violence.
